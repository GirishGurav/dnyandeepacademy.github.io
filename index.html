About Academy

            The Dnyandeep Academy was established in the city of Sangli in 2014 with the motto of 'Learn, Practise & Perform'. It has both CBSE & State Board curriculum coaching from grade 7 to 10. Ours is the unique academy in Sangli which teaches all subjects under one roof. We provide our students with tools like practice exams, study guides, and individual tutoring to help them better understand their strengths and areas for improvement. We also place an emphasis on developing other abilities that are crucial for outstanding academic success, such as speed, accuracy, and time management. Students who take advantage of our academy have a greater probability of academic success.
